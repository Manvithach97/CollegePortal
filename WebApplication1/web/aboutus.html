About Us

Welcome to BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for Women

BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for Women, located in Bachupally, is a premier institution under the esteemed Vishnu Educational Society. Established with the vision to empower women through quality education and to foster leadership and innovation, our college stands as a beacon of excellence in engineering education.

Our Vision

We aim to be a globally recognized institute for engineering education and research, fostering women engineers who will be leaders in technology, entrepreneurship, and service to society.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education that transforms students into competent professionals.
  • To encourage innovation and research in cutting-edge technologies.
  • To cultivate a culture of continuous learning and professional growth.
  • To empower women to excel in their chosen fields and contribute to society.

About Vishnu Educational Society

Vishnu Educational Society, founded by Padmabhushan Dr. B.V. Raju, is dedicated to providing world-class education and fostering research and development across various disciplines. The society manages a range of educational institutions, including engineering, pharmacy, and management colleges, all committed to excellence and innovation.

Our Commitment to Students

At BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for Women, we are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive environment that encourages learning and personal growth. Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and strong industry connections ensure that our students receive the best education and placement opportunities.

Join Us

We invite you to be a part of our vibrant community and embark on a journey of academic excellence and personal empowerment. Together, let's build a future where women engineers lead the way in innovation and technology.
